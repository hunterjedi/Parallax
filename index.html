Parallax Website

What is a Parallax Website

A parallax website is a website that uses a visual technique called parallax scrolling to create a sense of movement,
        depth, and interactivity:
        How it works
        The background of a parallax website moves more slowly than the foreground elements as you scroll, creating the illusion
        of depth and space. This is based on the optical illusion that objects that are closer to us appear larger than those
        that are farther away.
        Benefits
        Parallax scrolling can enhance the user experience by making a website more engaging and dynamic. It can also help guide
        visitors through content, draw their attention to specific areas, or make complex information easier to understand.
